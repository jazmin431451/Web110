Project Management

Internet project managers oversee all aspects of a technological project, including funding and scheduling. Continue reading to discover about typical job responsibilities, educational and training requirements, and career opportunities. Find out how much money this job can pay. Internet project managers supervise Internet-based efforts for clients, who can be individuals or businesses. It will be your role to work with the customer to understand their project goals, plan how to execute the project, implement it, and see it through to completion. You may interact with other IT professionals, such as computer software or hardware designers and computer support specialists, to ensure that the project is completed correctly and successfully. A project is usually made up of several activities or tasks. A work breakdown structure (WBS) is a hierarchical diagram of a project's duties. This subject describes how to establish a work breakdown structure (WBS), add tasks to it, and add requirements and other information to each task. It also explains how to work with income and costs in a WBS, as well as how to work with anticipated and actual hours for jobs in a WBS and how to use a WBS in Microsoft Project.
